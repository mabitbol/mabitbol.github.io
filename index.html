About me

Hi, I'm Max, I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Oxford. I study the physics of the early universe by analyzing light from the big bang, called the cosmic microwave background (CMB). One of my main research interests is to learn about inflation, a short period of exponential expansion of spacetime thought to have occured right at the beginning of the universe. To this end I am a part of two major CMB telescope collaborations: the Simons Observatory and the Atacama Cosmology Telescope.
